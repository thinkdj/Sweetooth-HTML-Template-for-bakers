Hi! I'm a Software-engineer-turned-baker. Never in my wildest dreams did I imagine myself baking cakes for a living! If someone had told me a few years back that I'd become a baker in the future, I would have scoffed at them. Yet, here I am, baking cakes!

I first got interested in cakes after watching cookery shows on television. My brother and cousins love to eat cakes. So, I thought why not give it a try... Gradually, I started baking cakes for my cousins' and friends' birthdays.

The very first cake I baked was burnt to crisp. To eat it, one would have to have been a pro at using a hammer and chisel! But that was the only cake I've burnt till date :)
